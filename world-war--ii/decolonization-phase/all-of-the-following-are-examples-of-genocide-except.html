DECOLONIZATION PHASE

[SOURCES]
All of the following are examples of genocide except.

(A) Rwanda

(B) Holocaust

(C) Cambodia

(D) ** WWI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Killing members of the group. Causing serious bodily or mental harm to members of the group. Deliberately inflicting on the group conditions of life calculated to bring about its physical destruction in whole or in part.

Concept note-2: -The definition contained in Article II of the Convention describes genocide as a crime committed with the intent to destroy a national, ethnic, racial or religious group, in whole or in part.

Concept note-3: -War, following Clausewitz, is the destruction by one armed actor of an armed enemy, its power and its will to resist. Genocide is the destruction by an armed actor of an unarmed social group, its power and its will to resist.

Concept note-4: -Expert-Verified Answer. Answer: the proper order of genocides from earliest to most recent-Armenian, Holocaust, Cambodian, Darfur.
