Following the end of hostilities in World War I, the American economy underwent a period of demobilization. Which is the BEST description of this process?

(A) ** returning soldiers re-entered the workforce and factories began to produce consumer goods again

(B) women and minority groups entered the workforce in record numbers to offset the effects of the draft

(C) government agencies were created to direct war-time production and conserve valuable resources such as iron and steel

(D) the United States Congress enacted a series of embargoes and tariffs to strengthen American businesses and industries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Stock prices collapsed first, by the end of 1919. The downturn in wholesale prices came 6 months later. By the autumn of 1920 a severe industrial depression had developed. Factory employment dropped 30 percent from March 1920 to July 1921.

Concept note-2: -Public demand quickly outpaced the slow production, leading to notable shortages of domestic goods. As a result, inflation skyrocketed in 1919. By the end of the year, the cost of living in the United States was nearly double what it had been in 1916.

Concept note-3: -What effect did the demobilization that followed World War I have on the American workforce? Thousands of women and African-Americans lost their jobs as returning servicemen reentered the work force.
