Which factor contributed to the use and spread of slavery in the thirteen colonies?

(A) ** Production of cash crops

(B) Proximity to natural harbors

(C) Lack of available immigrant labor

(D) Demand for manufactured goods

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The history and growth of slavery in colonial America was tied to the rise of land cultivation, and particularly the boom in the production of tobacco (in Virginia and Maryland) and rice (in the Carolinas).

Concept note-2: -European settlers brought a system of slavery with them to the western hemisphere in the 1500s. Unable to find cheap labor from other sources, white settlers increasingly turned to slaves imported from Africa. By the early 1700s in British North America, slavery meant African slavery.

Concept note-3: -Within the southern colonies of America between 1607 and 1775 factors such as available farm land, the increased production of agricultural crops, and general need for a stable labor force led to the development of slavery.
