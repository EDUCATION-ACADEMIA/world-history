What can be defined as a “political practice”?

(A) An economic policy in which a country accumulates riches by exploiting resources

(B) ** A system in which a state governs it’s citizens and individuals co-exist and abide by state laws

(C) A military strategy into controlling certain territories for national protection.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Democracy. Democracy, meaning “rule of the people", is a system of government in which the citizens exercise power directly or elect representatives from among themselves to form a governing body, such as a parliament.

Concept note-2: -In India, the state governments are the level of governments below the Union Government of India. India is a Sovereign Secular Democratic Republic with a Parliamentary system of government. The Republic is governed in terms of the Constitution of India.

Concept note-3: -political system, the set of formal legal institutions that constitute a “government” or a “state.” This is the definition adopted by many studies of the legal or constitutional arrangements of advanced political orders.

Concept note-4: -Totalitarianism is a form of government and a political system that prohibits all opposition parties, outlaws individual and group opposition to the state and its claims, and exercises an extremely high if not complete degree of control and regulation over public and private life.
