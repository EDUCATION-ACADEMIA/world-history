Which of the following was not an advantage of owning Hawaii, Guam, and the Philippines?

(A) they served as places to stop and refuel

(B) they made it easier to trade with Southeast Asia

(C) ** they served as a source of more workers for the large number of factories in the US

(D) they served as a military advantage in defending the US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How did Filipino leaders respond to the Philippines becoming a US territory? They started a rebellion.

Concept note-2: -The arguments in favor of imperialism were economic benefits, military strength and national superiority.

Concept note-3: -Hawaii was attractive to American imperialists for two reasons. First, its natural resources, especially sugar, could be exploited. Second, it was an ideal location for a naval base.

Concept note-4: -Which best describes the difference of opinion over the annexation of Hawaii? Some leaders felt annexation would benefit both countries, while others argued it was illegal.
