THE AGE OF IMPERIALISM 1870 1914

[SOURCES]
One advantage European countries had in their imperial expansion was

(A) weak economies

(B) absolutely no resistance on the part of Africans

(C) poorly organized governments

(D) ** powerful armies and navies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Europeans possessed more advanced military technology.

Concept note-2: -Imperialism benefited developing nations from the positive aspects of technological advancements, economic gain, and political power. European imperialism played a significant role of the evolution of transportation, military, and communication technologies.

Concept note-3: -Motives for European Imperialism. The three main motivators of European imperialism were economic growth, rivalry between nations, and moral superiority.

Concept note-4: -Imperialism offered any country a chance to make more money with access to raw materials and new markets. Controlling territory in another part of the world could keep strong countries strong by discouraging enemies with a global military presence.
