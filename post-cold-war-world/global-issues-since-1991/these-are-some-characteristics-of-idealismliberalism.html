These are some characteristics of Idealism/Liberalism

(A) ** COOPERATION as the main tool

(B) ** Cooperation is used to satisfy the necessities of ALL the States and actors in the International Arena.

(C) ** War and conflicts being totally avoided.

(D) ** States are totally trustworthy. Trust.

(E) State-centric (focused on States)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The essential orientation of idealism can be sensed through some of its typical tenets: “Truth is the whole, or the Absolute”; “to be is to be perceived”; “reality reveals its ultimate nature more faithfully in its highest qualities (mental) than in its lowest (material)”; “the Ego is both subject and object.”

Concept note-2: -Idealism is centered on the notion that states are rational actors capable of ensuring lasting peace and security rather than resorting to war. Idealism is also marked by the prominent role played by international law and international organizations in its conception of policy formation.

Concept note-3: -The underlying principle of idealism is that reality is largely an extension of mental processes, which are the true reality. Idealism proposes that ideas are universal and eternal, unlike physical objects, which are subject to the alteration of the forces of nature.
