RUSSIAN REVOLUTION

MAJOR EVENTS IN PRE REVOLUTION RUSSIA

[SOURCES]
This word referred to the workers in Russia.

(A) boyers

(B) the Whites

(C) ** proletariat

(D) Romanovs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marx defined the proletariat as the social class having no significant ownership of the means of production (factories, machines, land, mines, buildings, vehicles) and whose only means of subsistence is to sell their labor power for a wage or salary.

Concept note-2: -In the theory of Karl Marx, the term proletariat designated the class of wage workers who were engaged in industrial production and whose chief source of income was derived from the sale of their labour power.

Concept note-3: -German social scientist Karl Marx described industrial capitalism in great detail. He provided his understanding of the means of production, capital and wage labor. He also coined the term proletariat to describe the entire class of wage workers who can only earn money by selling their labor.

Concept note-4: -A proletarian revolution or proletariat revolution is a social revolution in which the working class attempts to overthrow the bourgeoisie and change the previous political system. Proletarian revolutions are generally advocated by socialists, communists and anarchists.
