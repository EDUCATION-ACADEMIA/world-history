what was NOT a purpose of the Congress of Vienna 1815?

(A) to place former monarchs back on their thrones

(B) create a balance of power in Europe after Napoleon’s defeat

(C) ** to place liberals in power across Europe

(D) to suppress democratic movements across Europe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The objective of the Congress was to provide a long-term peace plan for Europe by settling critical issues arising from the French Revolutionary Wars and the Napoleonic Wars through negotiation.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. United States of America was not a part of Vienna Congress of 1815. The Treaty of Vienna of 1815 was the formal agreement of the allied powers – Austria, Great Britain, Prussia and Russia to draw up a settlement for Europe.

Concept note-3: -Therefore, we can say that under the Treaty of Vienna of 1815, monarchies were restored and various state boundaries of French states. Thus, the correct answer is Option (D) i.e, Diluting the German Confederations of 39 states.

Concept note-4: -The Congress of Vienna and subsequent Congresses constituted a major turning point – the first genuine attempt to forge an ‘international order’, to bring long-term peace to a troubled Europe, and to control the pace of political change through international supervision and intervention.
