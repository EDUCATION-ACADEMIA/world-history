HOW DID THE AMERICAN REVOLUTION INFLUENCE THE FRENCH REVOLUTION

[SOURCES]
What was a major consequence of the American and French Revolutions?

(A) Protection of private property encouraged industrialization

(B) ** democratic reforms encouraged citizens to participate in government

(C) belief in equality led to economic reforms

(D) commitment to territorial expansion led to colonization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Revolution precipitated a series of European wars, forcing the United States to articulate a clear policy of neutrality in order to avoid being embroiled in these European conflicts.

Concept note-2: -The French Revolution eventually saw the execution of King Louis XVI. The abolishment of constitutional monarchy paved the way for the first Declaration of Human Rights, known as the Declaration of the Rights of Man and the Citizen.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: From the French and American Revolution we get idea of separation of powers among the three branches of government which is essential for democracy. It also generated the ideas of liberty, equality and fraternity.

Concept note-4: -Political reforms that were the result of the French revolution were the implementation of a constitution, the elimination of a feudal-like system and the introduction of a representative government. People, at least in theory, were equal and had the right to property.
