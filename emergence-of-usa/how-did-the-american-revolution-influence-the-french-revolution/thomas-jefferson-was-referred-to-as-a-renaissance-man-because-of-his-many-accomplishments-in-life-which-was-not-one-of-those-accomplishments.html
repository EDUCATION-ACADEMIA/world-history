Thomas Jefferson was referred to as a Renaissance Man because of his many accomplishments in life. Which was NOT one of those accomplishments?

(A) Founder of the University of Virginia-the first secular university in America

(B) 3rd President of America

(C) Author the Declaration of Independence

(D) ** Author of the Bill of Rights

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the Constitution’s framers heeded Thomas Jefferson who argued: “A bill of rights is what the people are entitled to against every government on earth, general or particular, and what no just government should refuse, or rest on inference."

Concept note-2: -He slashed Army and Navy expenditures, cut the budget, eliminated the tax on whiskey so unpopular in the West, yet reduced the national debt by a third. He also sent a naval squadron to fight the Barbary pirates, who were harassing American commerce in the Mediterranean.

Concept note-3: -The Louisiana Purchase doubled the size of the United States and is considered one of President Thomas Jefferson’s greatest presidential accomplishments.

Concept note-4: -Thomas Jefferson hoped that he would be remembered for three accomplishments: his founding of the University of Virginia, his crafting of the Virginia Statute for Religious Freedom, and his authorship of the Declaration of Independence.
