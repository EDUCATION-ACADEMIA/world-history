HOW DID THE AMERICAN REVOLUTION INFLUENCE THE FRENCH REVOLUTION

[SOURCES]
Which accurately summarizes the Continental Army’s victory at Trenton the day after Christmas, 1776?

(A) It ended the involvement of the German Hessians in the Revolutionary War.

(B) ** The Continental Army obtained needed food, weapons and confidence that helped them win two days later at Princeton.

(C) It allowed Washington to escape and fight another day

(D) It ended the Revolutionary War for the Continental Army

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -9 a.m. to 4 p.m. Despite the large number of Hessians that escaped Trenton, Washington still won a crucial strategic and material victory. In only one hour of fighting, the Continental Army captured nearly nine hundred Hessian officers and soldiers as well as a large supply of muskets, bayonets, swords, and cannons.

Concept note-2: -New Jersey | Dec 26, 1776. After crossing the Delaware River in a treacherous storm, General George Washington’s army defeated a garrison of Hessian mercenaries at Trenton. The victory set the stage for another success at Princeton a week later and boosted the morale of the American troops.

Concept note-3: -The victories reasserted American control of much of New Jersey and greatly improved the morale and unity of the colonial army and militias.

Concept note-4: -It was followed by two more American victories, first in a second battle at Trenton on January 2, 1777, and then on January 3 at Princeton.
