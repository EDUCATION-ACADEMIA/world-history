A political philosophy based on tradition and social stability, favoring obedience to political authority and organized religion.

(A) ** conservatism

(B) taille

(C) Principle of Intervention

(D) nationalism

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Conservatism is a cultural, social, and political philosophy that seeks to promote and to preserve traditional institutions, practices, and values. The central tenets of conservatism may vary in relation to the culture and civilization in which it appears.

Concept note-2: -Conservatism (Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy)

Concept note-3: -Conservatism: A political philosophy that stressed the importance of tradition, established Institutions and customs, preferred gradual development to quick change.

Concept note-4: -Socialism is a social and political philosophy based on the belief that democratic means should be used to distribute wealth evenly throughout a society. Communism is a political system characterized by a centrally planned economy with all economic and political power resting in the hands of the government.

Concept note-5: -November 2021) The principle of intervention in the context of European History was the Idea that the great powers have the right to use military force to restore order in countries threatened by revolution.
