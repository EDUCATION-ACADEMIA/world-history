Concept note-1: -The myth that Betsy Ross created the first American flag took off in the popular imagination and was enshrined in paintings like this Henry Mosler work, “The Birth of the Flag, ” depicting Ross and her assistants sewing in the Philadelphia house where she may not have actually lived.

Concept note-2: -Betsy would often tell her children, grandchildren, relatives, and friends of a fateful day, late in May of 1776, when three members of a secret committee from the Continental Congress came to call upon her. Those representatives, George Washington, Robert Morris, and George Ross, asked her to sew the first flag.
