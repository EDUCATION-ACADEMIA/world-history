a sudden overthrow of the government

(A) ** Coup d’etat

(B) Elba

(C) Waterloo

(D) Corsica

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: A sudden overthrow of government is called a coup (pronounced ‘koo’) or even a coup d’etat (pronounced ‘koo deh-tah’).

Concept note-2: -Brazilian Revolution of 1930: An armed revolution culminated in a coup d’état which ousted President Washington Luís and established the Brazilian military junta of 1930.

Concept note-3: -Coup or an overthrow, is the illegal and overt seizure of a State by the military or other elites within the state apparatus.

Concept note-4: -Coup d’état means “a sudden attempt by a small group of people to take over the government usually through violence.” But in its second sense, coup means “an impressive victory or achievement that usually is difficult or unexpected": It was a major coup when the team traded for the star pitcher.
