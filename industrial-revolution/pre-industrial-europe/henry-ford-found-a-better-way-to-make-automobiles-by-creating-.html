Henry Ford found a better way to make automobiles by creating ____

(A) the cotton gin.

(B) ** the assembly line.

(C) a better process for making steel.

(D) a steam engine.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What made this assembly line unique was the movement element. Henry Ford famously remarked that the use of the moving assembly line allowed for the work to be taken to workers rather than the worker moving to and around the vehicle. The vehicle began to be pulled down the line and built step-by-step.

Concept note-2: -Forget the Model T-Ford’s real innovation was the moving assembly line. It didn’t just usher in the age of the car; it changed work forever. He first fully implemented his innovation on December 1, 1913.

Concept note-3: -On December 1, 1913, Henry Ford installs the first moving assembly line for the mass production of an entire automobile. His innovation reduced the time it took to build a car from more than 12 hours to one hour and 33 minutes.
