“New tools, fertilizers, and harvesting techniques from the Agricultural Revolution increased productivity and resulted in a larger population.” What is the cause in this sentence?

(A) ** New tools, fertilizers, harvesting techniques came from the Agricultural Revolution

(B) A larger population resulted from new farming techniques

(C) The farming techniques allowed farmers to plant more

(D) The Industrial Revolution began in 1750.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Drones. Drones are the future of agriculture. Equipped with technology-based sensors, drones can be used in precision agriculture – monitoring crop health, weed and pests detection, crop scouting, analysis of soil health, irrigation management and livestock management among others.

Concept note-2: -These changes and technical innovations occurred over a long period of time, but together they represented a remarkable leap forward. Increasing temperatures opened the door for humans to learn how to cultivate wild plants, while new tools allowed humans to better manage crops and increase crop yields.

Concept note-3: -The agricultural revolution had a variety of consequences for humans. It has been linked to everything from societal inequality-a result of humans’ increased dependence on the land and fears of scarcity-to a decline in nutrition and a rise in infectious diseases contracted from domesticated animals.

Concept note-4: -For instance, the agro-industries in India have given a major boost to agriculture by raising its productivity.
