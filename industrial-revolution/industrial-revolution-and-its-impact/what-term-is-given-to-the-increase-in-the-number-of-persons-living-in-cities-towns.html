What term is given to the increase in the number of persons living in cities/ towns?

(A) Socialization

(B) ** Urbanization

(C) Population Growth

(D) Industrialization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -urbanization, the process by which large numbers of people become permanently concentrated in relatively small areas, forming cities.

Concept note-2: -Urbanisation is the increase in the proportion of people living in towns and cities. Urbanisation occurs because people move from rural areas (countryside) to urban areas (towns and cities). This usually occurs when a country is still developing.

Concept note-3: -Urbanization (or urbanisation) refers to the population shift from rural to urban areas, the corresponding decrease in the proportion of people living in rural areas, and the ways in which societies adapt to this change.

Concept note-4: -Urbanisation is an increase in the number of people living in towns and cities.

Concept note-5: -The correct option is D Urbanisation. Urbanisation means the process of concentration of the human population settling in an urban area. ‘Urban area’ refers to towns, cities, and suburbs.
